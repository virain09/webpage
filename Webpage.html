Rohit Malhotra's Interview


Rohit Malhotra, a fresh M.B.A. from ABC busuness School recieved an interview call from an MNC for the position of an intern. He has to appear for the interview on June 5 at 10 AM. at the company's Gurgaon Office. Rohit is excited about th eoppurtunity and begins preparing for the the interview.

The day of the intterview arrives. Rohit, who resides in Noida starts from home at 8:30 AM. However, he gets caught in a traffic jam and gets late for the interview. He arrives at the office at 10:30 AM. This unfactored delay make him anxious and nervous. After apologising to the receptionist and informing her of the reason for being late, Rohit waits for his turn for the interview.
